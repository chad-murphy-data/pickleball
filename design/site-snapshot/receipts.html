Calibration

When the frozen model said a team should win X% of the time, how often did they actually win? Dots on the diagonal = honest probabilities. Curve measured out-of-sample: every post-June-2026 game priced by the frozen pre-June fit (885 games). The fitted correction is nearly the identity (slope 0.90) — the earlier v1 model ran underconfident, this one doesn't. What the data does insist on: across all 36k games, favorites priced above 99% still lost 44 of 4248 games (~1%), so site probabilities are floored — nothing is ever 0% or 100%. Frozen predictions in the ledger above are graded exactly as committed, never re-calibrated after the fact.
